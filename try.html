Method Name Description
update_fingers_status() Updates finger status based on landmarks detected.
get_position() Calculates the position of the cursor based on the current hand position detected.
cursor_moving() Moves the cursor based on the position of a specific landmark on the hand.
detect_scrolling() Detects if the hand gesture indicates scrolling up or down and performs scrolling actions accordingly.
detect_zoomming() Detects if the hand gesture indicates zooming in or out and performs corresponding actions, such as simulating Ctrl+scroll.
detect_clicking() Detects left clicks, right clicks, and double clicks based on specific finger positions and performs corresponding actions using pyautogui.
detect_dragging() Detects if all fingers are down and initiates a dragging action accordingly.
adjust_volume() Adjusts the system volume based on thumbs-up or thumbs-down gestures.
take_screenshot_if_three_fingers_up() Takes a screenshot if three fingers are up and none of the other fingers are raised.
